Why painting and drawing ? and why our site ?

Painting and drawing are not merely hobbies or ways to pass the time; they are fundamental forms of human expression and hold significant value in numerous aspects of our lives and society. From an individual perspective, engaging in painting and drawing fosters creativity, enhances problem-solving skills, and provides a powerful outlet for emotional expression. The act of visualizing an idea and translating it onto a surface requires innovative thinking and the ability to make countless artistic decisions. This process strengthens cognitive flexibility and can be applied to various challenges in life. Furthermore, the focused nature of artistic creation can be meditative, reducing stress and anxiety while promoting a sense of accomplishment and self-esteem. The tangible result of a painting or drawing serves as a personal record of one's thoughts, feelings, and experiences, creating a visual diary that can be revisited and cherished.

Here we examine and explain paintings and drawings to learn more about art! Because art never dies...
